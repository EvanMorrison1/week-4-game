Carbiner Collector

Scenario: You are going on a climbing trip and need to bring a certain number of carbiners. The game will give you a number of carbiners, your job is to match the number.
There are four carbiners below. By clicking on a carbiner you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes over the random number.
The value of each carbiner is hidden from you until you click on it.
Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each carbiner.
